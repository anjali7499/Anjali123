Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

The Great Maratha Warrior

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj was the founder of the Maratha Empire in western India. He was known for his valor, military tactics, and administration.

Early Life

Shivaji Maharaj was born on 19th February 1630 in the Shivneri Fort. His mother, Jijabai, played a crucial role in shaping his character.

Achievements

  • Established a strong navy and forts along the western coast.
  • Introduced effective guerrilla warfare tactics.
  • Coronated as Chhatrapati in 1674 at Raigad Fort.
  • Promoted the use of Marathi and Sanskrit in administration.

Legacy

Shivaji Maharaj is remembered as a hero who fought against injustice and established a self-rule. His legacy continues to inspire millions.
